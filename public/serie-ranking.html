THE BEST SERIES I HAVE SEEN

My top 3 series of all time

The Expanse

A captivating sci-fi series that skillfully navigates complex politics, human nature, and space mysteries to deliver an enthralling narrative.

Jericho

"Jericho" is a suspenseful and thought-provoking series that explores the aftermath of a devastating nuclear attack on a small town, delving into human resilience and societal challenges.

Full Metal Alchemist: Brotherhood

"Fullmetal Alchemist: Brotherhood" combines intricate world-building, compelling characters, and philosophical themes, resulting in a spellbinding anime that leaves a lasting impact.
